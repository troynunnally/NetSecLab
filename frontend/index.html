The NetSecLab
gives you experience

The NetSecLab is conducted on a closed network with student-formed teams, each with their own Linux system to defend and from which to launch attacks. The lab was designed to familiarize students with Linux security and attacks. Students will learn how to:

  • Install the specified Linux distribution
  • Exploring attack & defense methods
  • Find ways to harden the team system and then compete
